# 🎮 Gloom & Bloom

## 📝 Overview

**Gloom & Bloom** is a story-driven adventure game where the player sets out on a simple mission—to bring flowers to his wife—but ends up lost in a mysterious and dangerous city.

What starts as a normal journey quickly turns into an exploration filled with secrets, unexpected events, and questionable characters.

---

## 🌆 Game Concept

The player navigates through a dark, foggy city, interacting with characters, collecting items, and completing tasks in order to progress.

Along the way, the player must:

* Find a hidden key 🔑
* Perform a street show to earn coins 🎤
* Trade with a police officer 👮‍♂️
* Finally obtain flowers 🌸

---

## 🎯 Gameplay Features

* 🚶‍♂️ Player movement in a 3D environment
* 🗣️ Interaction with NPCs
* 🎒 Item collection system
* 💬 Dialogue and decision-making
* 🎭 Simple mission-based progression

---

## ⚠️ Unexpected Twists

* The player accidentally enters a dark and dangerous city
* A mysterious phone call warns them to leave
* Characters may mislead the player
* Even after completing tasks, new challenges appear

---

## 🎮 Main Objective

Complete all tasks and obtain the flowers to return to your original goal.

---

## 🧩 Game Flow

1. Enter the mysterious city
2. Talk to the police officer
3. Search for the key
4. Deliver the key
5. Perform a street show to earn 200 coins
6. Trade coins for flowers
7. Complete the mission

---
